The Qumis dashboard is your central hub for coverage intelligence work. Understanding its layout and key sections will help you navigate efficiently and access the tools you need.

## Dashboard Overview

The Qumis interface is organized into three main areas:

**Sidebar Navigation** (left)
Your primary navigation menu, organized by feature area. Use it to move between major sections and access your workspace.

**Main Workspace** (center)
The primary working area where you'll interact with Qumis features, view results, and perform your coverage intelligence tasks.

**Top Bar** (top right)
Quick access to your account settings, notifications, and help resources.

## Key Areas and Features

### Chats

Your conversation hub for interactive analysis with Qumis.

- Start new analysis conversations with natural language prompts
- Reference documents and policies in your questions
- Build on previous conversations for deeper analysis
- Export chat summaries for reporting

[Learn more: Chats](/platform/chats)

### Reports

Generate, manage, and organize comprehensive coverage analysis reports.

- Create structured reports from chat analysis
- Save report templates for recurring analyses
- Export reports in multiple formats (PDF, Word, Excel)
- Share reports with team members and stakeholders

[Learn more: Reports](/platform/what-can-you-do)

### Vault

Your secure document repository for policies, claim files, and reference materials.

- Upload and organize insurance documents
- Tag documents for easy searching and filtering
- Version control for document iterations
- Search document content across your entire vault

[Learn more: Document Vault](/platform/document-vault)

### Legal Search

Research case law and legal precedents relevant to coverage questions.

- Search by jurisdiction, coverage type, or legal issue
- Access summaries of relevant cases
- Filter results by date, court level, and ruling type
- Link findings to your coverage analysis

[Learn more: Legal Search](/platform/research/legal-search)

### Prompt Library

Browse and save your most effective analysis prompts for reuse.

- Save prompts you develop for common analysis tasks
- Organize prompts by category or coverage type
- Quickly launch analyses with saved prompts
- Share effective prompts with your team

[Learn more: Prompt Library](/platform/prompt-library)

## Quick Actions

### Start a New Chat

Click the "New Chat" button (typically in the sidebar or top menu) to begin a new analysis conversation. You can reference documents from your Vault and ask coverage questions in natural language.

### Upload a Document

Use the "Upload" option to add policies, claims, or reference documents to your Vault. Documents are processed and made searchable immediately.

### Generate a Report

Once you've completed chat analysis, convert your findings into a formal report using the "Generate Report" feature. Select your analysis from recent chats and choose a report template.

## Tips for Efficient Navigation

- **Bookmark frequent sections**: Most Qumis interfaces allow you to favorite or bookmark sections you use regularly
- **Use search**: If you're looking for a specific document, chat, or report, use the search function rather than browsing
- **Organize with tags**: Apply consistent tags to documents and reports to make them easier to find later
- **Check notifications**: The top bar shows notifications about report completion, shared content, and system updates
