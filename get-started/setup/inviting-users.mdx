Quickly onboard your team to Qumis by inviting users and assigning appropriate roles based on their responsibilities and access needs.

## Inviting Users

Follow these steps to add a new user to your Qumis organization:

<Steps>
<Step title="Navigate to Team Settings">
From the main dashboard, go to Settings and select Team from the menu.
</Step>

<Step title="Click Invite User">
Select the "Invite User" or "Add Team Member" button.
</Step>

<Step title="Enter Email Address">
Type the email address of the person you want to invite. This should be the email they'll use to log into Qumis.
</Step>

<Step title="Assign a Role">
Choose the appropriate role for this user based on their responsibilities:
- **Admin**: Full platform access including settings, team management, and billing
- **Member**: Full platform access to all analysis features, documents, and reports
- **Viewer**: Read-only access to shared reports and documents (cannot create new analysis or upload documents)

See the role definitions below for details.
</Step>

<Step title="Send Invitation">
Click "Send Invitation." The user will receive a welcome email with login instructions.
</Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
New users will receive a welcome email with login instructions. If your organization uses SSO, they'll be directed to your identity provider.
</Tip>

## User Roles and Permissions

Choose the right role for each team member:

### Admin

**Best for**: Team leads, organizational administrators, compliance officers

**Permissions include:**
- Full access to all Qumis features (Chats, Reports, Vault, Legal Search, Prompt Library)
- Invite and manage team members
- Configure SSO and identity provider settings
- Access billing and subscription information
- Manage organization-level settings and integrations

### Member

**Best for**: Coverage counsel, underwriters, claims analysts, risk managers

**Permissions include:**
- Create and manage chats and analysis conversations
- Generate and share reports
- Upload and organize documents in the Vault
- Access Legal Search and Prompt Library
- View and comment on shared reports from other members
- Cannot access team management or billing settings

### Viewer

**Best for**: Stakeholders, external counsel, executives needing read-only access

**Permissions include:**
- View shared reports
- Access shared documents
- Review chat summaries and analyses
- Cannot create new chats or upload documents
- Cannot modify any content

## Bulk Invitations

If you're adding multiple users at once, contact your Qumis account team. They can facilitate bulk user imports from your identity provider (especially useful with SSO enabled) or process multiple invitations at once.

## First Login Experience

When a new user logs in for the first time, they'll see:

1. A welcome screen with key Qumis features explained
2. An option to tour the dashboard
3. Links to this documentation and onboarding resources
4. Quick-start prompts to begin their first analysis

New members can jump into coverage analysis immediately or explore the dashboard at their own pace.

## Managing Existing Users

From the Team settings page, you can:
- **Change user roles**: Select a user and update their role
- **Deactivate access**: Remove a user's access without deleting their content
- **View activity**: See login history and platform usage by team member
- **Resend invitations**: Resend the welcome email if a user didn't receive it

## Best Practices

- **Assign appropriate roles**: Use Viewer role for stakeholders who only need read-only access to reduce license costs
- **Keep SSO in sync**: If using SSO, ensure your identity provider and Qumis are synchronized when users leave your organization
- **Document role assignments**: Maintain a record of who has which role for compliance and access audits
- **Review permissions regularly**: Audit team member roles quarterly to ensure access remains appropriate
