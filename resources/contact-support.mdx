The Qumis support team is here to help you get the most from the platform. Here's how to reach us and what to include in your support request.

## Getting in Touch

### Email Support

Reach out to us at:

**support@qumis.ai**

Include as much detail as possible (see below) to help us resolve your issue faster.

## What to Include in a Support Request

To help our team assist you more efficiently, provide the following information:

**Essential Information:**
- Clear description of the issue or question
- Steps you took that led to the problem
- What you expected to happen vs. what actually happened
- Your username or email associated with your Qumis account

**Helpful Additions:**
- Screenshots or screen recordings of the issue
- Document names or IDs referenced in your question
- Browser name and version (if relevant to the issue)
- Date and time the issue occurred
- Any error messages you received

## Feature Requests and Feedback

We value your input. To submit a feature request or general feedback, email **support@qumis.ai** with the subject line "Feature Request:" and a description of your suggestion.

Your feedback helps shape the future of Qumis coverage intelligence capabilities.
