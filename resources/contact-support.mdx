The Qumis support team is here to help you get the most from the platform. Here's how to reach us and what to include in your support request.

## Getting in Touch

### In-App Support Chat

The quickest way to get help is through the in-app chat widget available in the bottom right corner of your Qumis dashboard. Our support team monitors this channel during business hours and typically responds within minutes.

### Email Support

For detailed issues or if you prefer email, reach out to:

**support@qumis.ai**

Include as much detail as possible (see below) to help us resolve your issue faster.

## What to Include in a Support Request

To help our team assist you more efficiently, provide the following information:

**Essential Information:**
- Clear description of the issue or question
- Steps you took that led to the problem
- What you expected to happen vs. what actually happened
- Your username or email associated with your Qumis account

**Helpful Additions:**
- Screenshots or screen recordings of the issue
- Document names or IDs referenced in your question
- Browser name and version (if relevant to the issue)
- Date and time the issue occurred
- Any error messages you received

## Response Times

We aim to respond to all support requests during business hours (Monday-Friday, 9 AM - 6 PM EST):

- **Urgent Issues** (system down, data loss): 1-2 hours
- **High Priority** (feature not working, blocking analysis): 4 hours
- **Standard** (how-to questions, general troubleshooting): 24 hours

For critical production issues outside business hours, indicate "Urgent" in your request and provide a phone number where you can be reached.

## Feature Requests and Feedback

We value your input. To submit a feature request or general feedback:

1. Use the feedback form in the Qumis app (typically accessible from the Help menu)
2. Email support@qumis.ai with subject line "Feature Request:"
3. Share your suggestion during a scheduled check-in with your account team

Your feedback helps shape the future of Qumis coverage intelligence capabilities.
