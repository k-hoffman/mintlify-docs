The Qumis support portal gives you real-time visibility into every ticket your team has submitted — including open requests, in-progress issues, and resolved items. You can track status, view responses from the support team, and submit new requests, all in one place.

## Access the support portal

<Card title="Open the Qumis Support Portal" href="https://support.qumis.ai">
  View your tickets, track status, and submit new requests in the Qumis support portal.
</Card>

<Info>
  The support portal uses your Qumis account credentials. If you're already logged into Qumis, you'll be authenticated automatically. If not, you'll be prompted to sign in.
</Info>

## What you can do in the portal

- **View open tickets** and their current status
- **Track progress** on in-progress issues with real-time updates
- **Review resolved tickets** and the solutions provided
- **Submit new support requests** directly from the portal
- **Search your ticket history** to find past resolutions

## Need help?

If you're having trouble accessing the portal or have questions about a ticket, reach out to the support team directly via the [Contact Support](/resources/contact-support) page.
