## Login & Access

### I Can't Log In to Qumis

**Problem:** You're receiving an error when trying to log in with your credentials.

**Solutions:**

- Verify you're using the correct email address associated with your Qumis account
- Check that your organization's SSO is properly configured if you're redirecting through an identity provider
- Try resetting your password using the "Forgot Password" link on the login page
- Clear your browser cache and cookies, then try again
- Ensure you're using a supported browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge)

### SSO Is Not Working

**Problem:** SSO authentication is failing or showing errors.

**Solutions:**

- Verify your identity provider is configured correctly and your account is active in that system
- Check that you're using the correct SSO login URL provided by Qumis for your organization
- Confirm your browser allows third-party cookies (required for SSO redirects)
- If redirects are looping, contact your IT administrator to verify SAML configuration
- See [Single Sign-On (SSO)](/get-started/setup/single-sign-on) for detailed setup and troubleshooting

### I Didn't Receive My Invitation Email

**Problem:** An admin invited you but you haven't received the welcome email.

**Solutions:**

- Check your spam or junk folder
- Request the admin resend your invitation from the Team settings page
- Verify the email address used for the invitation matches your email address
- If you were added directly via SSO, you may not receive an email—try logging in with your identity provider credentials

### I Can't Reset My Password

**Problem:** The password reset process isn't working.

**Solutions:**

- Verify you're using the correct email address
- Check your spam folder for the password reset email
- If using SSO, you cannot reset your password in Qumis—manage your password through your identity provider
- Contact [Support](/resources/contact-support) if reset emails aren't arriving

## Documents

### Document Upload Fails

**Problem:** You're unable to upload a document to the Vault.

**Solutions:**

- Verify the file size is under the 50 MB limit (contact support for larger files)
- Check that the file format is supported: PDF, DOCX, PPTX, TXT, XLS, XLSX
- Try uploading from a different browser if you suspect browser issues
- Ensure your browser has permission to upload files
- Try a different document to rule out file-specific issues

### Document Is Not Processing

**Problem:** You uploaded a document but it's still showing as "Processing" after several minutes.

**Solutions:**

- Refresh your browser page—processing may be complete
- Check document file size and complexity (large or heavily scanned documents take longer)
- Verify your internet connection is stable
- For scanned PDFs with poor image quality, document processing may take longer
- Wait 5-10 minutes, then contact [Support](/resources/contact-support) if the issue persists

### Unsupported File Format Error

**Problem:** You're getting an error that your file format isn't supported.

**Solutions:**

- Qumis supports: PDF, DOCX, PPTX, TXT, XLS, XLSX
- If your document is in a different format (e.g., JPG, PNG), convert it to PDF first
- For scanned images, convert them to PDF using your device's scanner app or online tools
- Contact [Support](/resources/contact-support) to request support for additional formats

### How Do I Handle Large Files?

**Problem:** Your document is larger than the standard upload limit.

**Solutions:**

- Split large documents into sections
- Remove unnecessary attachments or images to reduce file size
- Contact [support@qumis.ai](mailto:support@qumis.ai) to request a temporary increase in upload limits for Enterprise customers

## Chats & Analysis

### Chat Responses Are Slow

**Problem:** Qumis is taking a long time to respond to your questions.

**Solutions:**

- This can occur during peak usage times—try again in a few moments
- Simplify your question or break it into smaller questions
- Ensure you've uploaded all relevant documents before asking questions about them
- Close other browser tabs to free up system resources
- Check your internet connection speed
- If slowness persists, contact [Support](/resources/contact-support)

### Analysis Results Seem Unexpected or Inaccurate

**Problem:** Chat responses don't match what you expected based on the documents provided.

**Solutions:**

- Verify the correct documents were uploaded and referenced in your question
- Ask follow-up questions to clarify or dive deeper into specific aspects
- Rephrase your question with more specific details or policy section references
- Reference [Tips and Best Practices](/platform/tips-and-best-practices) for guidance on writing effective prompts
- If results are clearly incorrect, include the conversation details when you [Contact Support](/resources/contact-support)

### How Do I Improve the Quality of Analysis Results?

**Problem:** You want better, more accurate responses from Qumis.

**Solutions:**

- Provide specific context: reference policy sections, coverage types, and claim scenarios
- Ask one focused question at a time rather than multiple questions in one prompt
- Use coverage-specific terminology and be precise about dates, amounts, and coverage limits
- Include all relevant documents in your Vault before analyzing
- See [Tips and Best Practices](/platform/tips-and-best-practices) for detailed guidance on effective prompt techniques

## Reports

### Report Generation Failed

**Problem:** You attempted to generate a report but received an error.

**Solutions:**

- Verify you have completed sufficient analysis in the chat to generate a report from
- Try generating the report again—temporary system issues may have caused the failure
- Use a simpler report template if available
- Reduce the amount of analysis content being converted to a report
- Contact [Support](/resources/contact-support) with details about the specific chat or report

### Report Has Formatting Issues

**Problem:** Your exported report has layout problems, missing content, or visual issues.

**Solutions:**

- Try exporting in a different format (PDF vs. Word vs. Excel)
- Check that all content appears in the preview before exporting
- If exporting to Word, open the file in Microsoft Word rather than other applications
- For PDF formatting issues, try opening in a different PDF reader
- Contact [Support](/resources/contact-support) if formatting issues persist

### Export Not Working

**Problem:** You cannot export a report or it's not saving to your computer.

**Solutions:**

- Check your browser's download settings—downloads may be blocked
- Disable browser extensions that might interfere with downloads (ad blockers, privacy extensions)
- Try a different file format for export
- Ensure your device has sufficient storage space
- Try exporting from a different browser if the issue persists

## Legal Search

### Legal Search Returns No Results

**Problem:** Your search for case law or precedents isn't finding anything relevant.

**Solutions:**

- Verify you've selected the correct jurisdiction for your search
- Try broader search terms if you used very specific language
- Ensure you're searching for coverage-related legal issues (not general law)
- Try different variations of coverage type or claim scenario
- Refine your search filters by court level or date range
- Contact [Support](/resources/contact-support) if you believe results are missing for a common legal issue

### Jurisdiction Selection

**Problem:** You're unsure which jurisdiction to select or can't find your state/territory.

**Solutions:**

- Legal Search covers U.S. federal courts and state appellate courts
- Select the specific state where the case or claim is relevant
- For federal cases, choose "Federal" and specify the circuit court if available
- Territory cases (Puerto Rico, Virgin Islands) are covered in their own jurisdiction section
- Contact [Support](/resources/contact-support) to request coverage for additional jurisdictions

### Search Results Don't Seem Relevant

**Problem:** Results are returned but don't match what you were looking for.

**Solutions:**

- Refine your search terms to be more specific to your coverage issue
- Check the case summary before opening full details
- Adjust date range filters to focus on recent precedents
- Try combining multiple search terms to narrow results
- Contact [Support](/resources/contact-support) if you find incorrect results or citation errors
