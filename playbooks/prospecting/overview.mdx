## Who these playbooks are for

These playbooks are designed for **producers and account executives** at commercial insurance brokerages who are actively prospecting for new business. They assume you have access to Qumis and are familiar with the basics of uploading documents, running chats, and generating reports.

<Note>
  If you're new to Qumis, start with the [Get Started](/get-started/welcome) guides before diving into these playbooks. You'll get more out of each workflow once you're comfortable navigating the platform.
</Note>

---

## How to use these playbooks

Each playbook follows the same structure:

- **The strategy** explains why this approach works and what makes it different from the way most brokers operate today.
- **The Qumis workflow** walks you through the exact steps, features, and outputs you'll use.
- **Templates and talk tracks** give you ready-to-use language for outreach, follow-up, and conversation.
- **Measuring success** defines what good looks like so you can track whether the playbook is working.

Pick the playbook that matches your next prospecting priority and work through it. You don't need to do all three at once.
