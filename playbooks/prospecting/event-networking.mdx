Stand out at industry conferences, association events, and networking functions by demonstrating live Qumis analysis instead of handing out capabilities decks. When a prospect can see their own coverage questions answered in real time, the conversation shifts from "what do you do?" to "how do I get access to this?"

---

## What you'll learn

This guide will walk you through preparing Qumis for live event demos, running real-time policy analysis in front of prospects, converting event conversations into follow-up meetings, and building a repeatable conference playbook for your team.
