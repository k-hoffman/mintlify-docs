Playbooks are guided workflows that show you how to combine Qumis tools to accomplish real insurance tasks — from analyzing a prospect's coverage program to preparing a client renewal presentation. Each playbook walks you through a specific scenario step by step, so you can follow along with your own documents and get results immediately.

---

## What's in a playbook?

Every playbook includes a clear goal, the Qumis tools you'll use, step-by-step instructions, and tips for getting the best results. Think of them as recipes — follow the steps, swap in your own documents, and adapt the workflow to your situation.

## Playbooks for your role

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Brokers & Producers">
    Start with the **Prospecting** playbooks to build pipeline, then use the **Broking** playbooks to market accounts, compare quotes, and verify bindings. The three broking playbooks follow the natural sequence of a placement.

    Key playbooks: [Referral Mining](/playbooks/prospecting/referral-mining) → [Renewal Outreach](/playbooks/prospecting/renewal-outreach) → [Marketing the Account](/playbooks/broking/marketing-account) → [Quote Comparison](/playbooks/broking/quote-comparison) → [Binding and Policy Review](/playbooks/broking/binding-review)
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Account Managers">
    Use the **Servicing** playbooks to deliver proactive value between renewals — from strategic stewardship meetings to fast certificate turnaround and real-time coverage answers. Then prepare with **Renewal Preparation** and **Renewal Analysis** when it's time.

    Key playbooks: [Stewardship Meetings](/playbooks/servicing/stewardship) → [Contracts & Certificates](/playbooks/servicing/contracts-certificates) → [Coverage Questions](/playbooks/servicing/coverage-questions) → [Renewal Preparation](/playbooks/renewing/renewal-preparation) → [Renewal Analysis](/playbooks/renewing/renewal-analysis)
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Claims Professionals">
    Start with **Coverage Determination** to build defensible coverage opinions, then use **Building Your Case** to support formal determinations with structured legal research.

    Key playbooks: [Coverage Determination](/platform/research/coverage-determination) → [Building Your Case](/platform/research/building-your-case)
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Risk Managers">
    Use **Stewardship Meetings** for program-level health checks, **Coverage Questions** for real-time answers on exposures, and **Binding and Policy Review** to verify new coverage before it's delivered.

    Key playbooks: [Stewardship Meetings](/playbooks/servicing/stewardship) → [Coverage Questions](/playbooks/servicing/coverage-questions) → [Binding and Policy Review](/playbooks/broking/binding-review)
  </Tab>
</Tabs>
