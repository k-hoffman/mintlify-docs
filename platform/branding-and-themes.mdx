Make every Qumis deliverable feel like it came from your team. Branding & Themes lets you apply your organization's visual identity to reports, exports, and client-facing documents.

---

## What's covered in this guide

This guide will walk you through:

- **Uploading your logo** — Add your company logo so it appears on generated reports and exports.
- **Setting brand colors** — Define primary and accent colors that carry through headers, charts, and highlights.
- **Report templates** — Configure default layouts for different report types so your team's output stays consistent.
- **Preview & test** — See how your branding looks before rolling it out across your organization.
