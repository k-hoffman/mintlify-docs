# Proposal Generator

Turn your Qumis analysis into polished, client-ready proposals — without starting from scratch. The Proposal Generator combines policy data, market research, and your branded templates to produce professional proposals in minutes.

---

## What to expect

- **Template-driven output** — Start from your organization's proposal templates, pre-populated with extracted policy data and analysis.
- **Integrated research** — Pull in coverage comparisons, market insights, and competitive positioning directly from your Qumis workspace.
- **Collaborative editing** — Review and refine generated proposals before sharing with clients.
- **Export options** — Download as Word or PDF, ready for client delivery.
