# Chrome Extension

Bring Qumis into your everyday browser workflow. The Chrome Extension lets you access document analysis, chat, and report generation directly from any web page — no need to switch to the Qumis dashboard.

---

## What to expect

- **In-browser analysis** — Highlight text on any page and send it to Qumis for instant analysis or comparison.
- **Quick chat** — Ask questions about your uploaded documents without leaving your current tab.
- **One-click reports** — Trigger report generation from your browser when reviewing documents in your DMS or email.
- **Seamless login** — Authenticate once with your existing Qumis SSO credentials.
