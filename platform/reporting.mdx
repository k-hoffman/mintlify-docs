# Reporting

The Reporting dashboard gives leadership and admins visibility into how Qumis is being used — so you can track adoption, identify power users, and demonstrate ROI.

---

## What's covered in this guide

This guide will walk you through:

- **Usage metrics** — See how many reports, chats, and extractions your team runs over time.
- **Adoption tracking** — Monitor which teams and individuals are actively using Qumis.
- **Time savings estimates** — Understand the efficiency gains Qumis delivers based on actual usage patterns.
- **Export & share** — Pull reporting data for executive summaries, board decks, or internal reviews.
