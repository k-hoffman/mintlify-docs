# Administration

The Administration console gives workspace owners and admins centralized control over users, permissions, and organizational settings.

---

## What's covered in this guide

This guide will walk you through:

- **User management** — Add, deactivate, and manage team members across your organization.
- **Roles & permissions** — Assign roles that control what each user can access and do within Qumis.
- **Teams & groups** — Organize users into teams for streamlined document sharing and collaboration.
- **Workspace settings** — Configure organization-wide defaults for reports, notifications, and data handling.
- **Audit log** — Review activity history to track usage and maintain compliance.
